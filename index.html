LIFE Argentina is a nonprofit, civil society organization that works with youth living in socially marginalized and extremely impoverished areas of Argentina.

WHO WE ARE

Our programs/activities are aimed at promoting the healthy development and a hopeful future for the children we serve.

WHERE WE WORK

We work collaboratively with community centers and soup kitchens in the city of Buenos Aires and its suburbs.

PARTNERSHIPS

Our services include providing recreational, educational and social activities as well as food, clothing and supplies.
